About Us

Don't waste your time looking for a parking spot , use our website!

Finding a parking spot during busy hours and when you're late is hard.

Most of the University of Jordan students have their own cars. When they come to the university, they are not sure if they will have a place to park at university parking. Where to Park is a website for the University of Jordan students to book their place 15 minutes before they arrive. This website will help students to make sure their place is booked and where it is.

Online Booking delivers so many benefits

Students are increasingly wanting to plan and pre-book their parking and are seeking hassle free parking experience.


Offering online booking as part of the university services and atract the students to the parkings.

Enable students to book parking. All bookings are verified and listed in View Booking tab you can see them by clicking on it.

"Where to Park" integrates the online booking technology with leading car park equipment providers to enable touch-free entry and exit.


We offer social sign-in to make it even easier for students to register and sign-in for repeat visits.


Access is being granted via credit card , students' name or vehicle number plate for identification.
